Step1 : Sow Corn seeds in well-drained soil rich in added organic manure like bio-compost or vermicompost.
Step2 : Water your potted corn plant every day in summer & every other day in winter.
Step3 : Water at the soil near the plant. Use a watering can make sure plants get water in the form of the shower & not by a single flow. You can also make provision for watering by drip irrigation system on your raised beds.
Step4 : Add good quality organic manure in the soil with 2:1 ratio before sowing seeds.
Step5 : Organic manure can be well rotten cow dung manure, farmyard manure, Compost or Vermicompost.
Step6 : corn plant will start flowering on of 3rd month;corn cobs can be harvested after 80-90 days of seed sowing.
Step7 : Corn cob should be harvested when seeds are well formed, but its skin can be punctured with a fingernail.
Step8 : A downward twisting jerk will usually snap the ear from the stalk.
